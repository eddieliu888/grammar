⚠️ 無敵魔王規則：
只要句子裡出現了 Do 或 Does，
後面的動詞就會「被打回原形」，絕對不可以加 s 或 es！

動詞超級變變變 (點擊看變化)

中英文語序大不同！

中文邏輯：[主詞] + 時間 + 地方 + 動作
英文邏輯：Who + Action + Where + When
主詞 + 動作 + 地方 + 時間

情境問答挑戰

Q: What does he like to do?

A: He likes to go hiking.

Q: What time do you get up?

A: I get up at 7:00.

Q: What time does she go to bed?

A: She goes to bed at 10:30.

Q: What do they do on Monday?

A: They play basketball on Monday.

Q: What does she do on Sunday?

A: She visits a friend on Sunday.

Q: What does John do on Saturday?

A: He flies a kite on Saturday.
